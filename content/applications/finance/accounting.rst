========================
Accounting and Invoicing
========================

**Flectra Invoicing** is a standalone invoicing app to create invoices, send them to your customers,
and manage payments.

**Flectra Accounting** is a full featured accounting app. Accountant productivity is at the core of its
development with features such as AI-powered invoice recognition, synchronization with your bank
accounts, smart matching suggestions, etc.
