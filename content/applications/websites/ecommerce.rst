=========
eCommerce
=========

Run a modern open-source online store with Flectra eCommerce. Learn how to sell online, promote
products and increase your average cart sizes.

.. important::
   Flectra offers a :ref:`free custom domain name <domain-name/flectra-register>` to all Flectra Online
   databases for one year. Visitors can then access your website with an address such as
   ``www.example.com`` rather than the default ``example.flectra.com``.
